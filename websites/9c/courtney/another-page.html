How to book:

When it comes to booking, there are a few simple steps that must be completed first.

First you can email us at waffle.basket@yahoo.com, or send us a text at 077 6057 4336 stating what date and time that you would like to dine with us.

Next, wait for 10-30 minutes for our response (we suggest that you should look at our dessert and drinks menu whilst you wait) and then we can confirm if your chosen date and time is available.

Finally, if your date is available, you can arrive at the store and come dine with us fully booked, and not needing to worry about any stress :)
