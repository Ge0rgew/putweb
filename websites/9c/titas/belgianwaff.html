Belgian Waffles

Belgian waffles, also known as gaufres de Bruxelles or gaufres de Liege in the original language, are a type of waffle that first originated in Liege, Belgium and is still a popular breakfast food in Belgium. The first recipe for this dish was created by a chef who lived in Brussels at the turn of the 18th century.
